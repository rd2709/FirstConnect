About First Connect

We're Not
Just Recruiters.

Our Story
Built by technologists.
For technology companies.

First Connect was built on one stubborn belief: the recruiting industry has fundamentally failed the AI and cloud engineering community. Generic HR firms keyword-match CVs. Job boards flood inboxes with irrelevant noise. The best engineers — the ones actually building real AI systems — aren't applying anywhere.

We exist because the people who need to hire — founders scaling AI functions, CTOs rebuilding cloud infrastructure, engineering leaders building DevOps practices from zero — deserve a partner who actually speaks their language. Not someone who Googled "Kubernetes" five minutes before the call.

"We don't just share resumes. We help you hire billable AI talent — people who walk in and start contributing from day one."

Our team has deep roots in the AI, cloud and DevOps ecosystem. We understand the difference between an MLOps Engineer and an AI Research Scientist. We know what LangChain, RAG pipelines and LLM fine-tuning actually mean in production. This fluency is what lets us identify talent others miss — and screen out candidates who look good on paper but can't deliver.

With 50+ clients and 300+ placements across the US and India — from Series A AI startups to publicly listed global enterprises — we've built something that didn't exist before: a talent intelligence partner purpose-built for the new age of AI-first organisations.
