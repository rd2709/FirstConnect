Our Story
Built by people who understand what great tech talent actually looks like.
300+
Successful Placements Across AI, Cloud & Leadership
8
C-Suite Placements in Last 8 Months

First Connect was founded on a frustration that too many people in this industry will recognise: exceptional companies were failing to hire exceptional talent — not because the talent didn't exist, but because the recruiters looking for it didn't understand it.

Generic HR firms keyword-match CVs. Job boards flood inboxes with irrelevant noise. And the best engineers, leaders, and specialists — the ones actually building real systems and driving real outcomes — weren't on any job board at all. They were employed, performing, and selectively open to the right opportunity if someone found them.

We're not a recruitment agency that does tech. We're a tech-first talent partner that happens to recruit — and the difference shows in every placement we make.

We've built four distinct specialities because different hiring problems require fundamentally different approaches. A Chief AI Officer search requires a completely different playbook than contract developer augmentation. We've mastered all four — with dedicated teams, live pipelines, and domain intelligence that generalist firms simply cannot replicate.

With 50+ clients, 300+ placements, 8 C-suite hires in 8 months, and a contract pool of 2000+ resources — First Connect is the talent partner for companies that are serious about building.
