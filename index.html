Who We Are

A Technology-First Recruitment Firm, Built for India

First Connect is a specialist technology recruitment firm built from the ground up for the Indian market. We are not a generalist agency with a tech practice — technology is our entire business, and every process, tool, and hire we make reflects that singular focus.

Our founders saw the same frustration repeated across engineering leaders in India: agencies that didn't understand the technology, sent volumes of irrelevant profiles, and disappeared after placement. First Connect was built to be the antidote to that experience.

Our recruiters come from backgrounds in technical hiring, software consulting, and technology operations. They've sat in architecture review meetings, run technical interviews, and understand what separates a 90-day churn from a 3-year contributor. That knowledge is embedded in every shortlist we deliver.

Today, with 600+ placements across India's most ambitious technology teams, over 78% of our business comes from repeat clients and referrals — a testament to outcomes, not promises.
